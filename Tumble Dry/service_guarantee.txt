# Service Guarantee & Quality Promise

## Quality Assurance
*   **Hygiene**: We use separate wash cycles for every customer. Your clothes are never mixed with others.
*   **Chemicals**: We use eco-friendly, German organic chemicals (Diversey) that are soft on clothes and skin.
*   **Technology**: American washing machines and Italian steam ironing equipment.
*   **Zero Shrinkage**: Guaranteed for curtain dry cleaning.

## Stain Removal
*   **Promise**: We strive for 99% stain removal.
*   **Process**: We use specialized spotting guns and chemicals for different stain types (oil, ink, rust, etc.).
*   **Safety**: If a stain is too deep, we stop cleaning to prevent fabric damage.

## Re-processing
*   If you are not happy with the cleaning quality, we will **re-process the item for free**.
